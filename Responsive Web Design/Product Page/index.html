About Us

We are a small company dedicated to maintaining and nurturing the art of book-binding. We have a deep appreciation for the vintage aesthetic of books as they were made in times past, and we wish to spread this passion with our books, all of which are crafted with love and attention to detail. It is our goal to craft books that are not only functional and highly durable, but also works of art that can be proudly displayed on a bookshelf.

Craftsmanship

  • Highest Quality Materials

    Our books are crafted with premium quality paper and locally-sourced sheepskin leather. The paint used is selected to be resistant to fading with time, and to be resistant to skin-oils. Gilding is done with real gold leaf.

  • Handcrafted with Attention to Detail

    Every book we craft for you is handmade using only hand-tools, and with a focus on detail. We believe that detail is what separates the ordinary from the extraordinary, and we aim to create a masterpiece with every single book we craft.

  • Express International Shipping

    Beautiful books should not, and cannot, be limited by borders! We accept orders from all over the world, and we offer express shipping options. Additionally, we make sure to carefully pack every order we send out, to ensure that no damage is done to your book on its way to you.
